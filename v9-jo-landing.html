FAQ

What is jo?

An AI assistant that runs half on your Mac, half on your own dedicated cloud machine. It searches your notes, photos, emails, messages, and files. Available on macOS, Telegram, and WhatsApp.

Does my data leave my Mac?

Personal data (photos, screen content, local files) never leaves your Mac. Conversations and tasks run on your dedicated cloud machine. Cloud inference uses providers that never train on your data.

What's a "dedicated machine"?

Every jo user gets their own cloud server. Not a container, not a row in a database. A real machine with its own storage that mounts as a drive on your Mac.

How does pricing work?

No subscription. Simple queries cost about 1 cent. Complex queries about 5 cents. Most users spend $5-10 per month. First month is free.

Which models can I use?

OpenAI, Anthropic, Grok, Kimi, and a local model that runs entirely on your Mac. Swap anytime. Jo auto-routes between fast and reasoning models based on the question.

How is jo different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT lives on someone else's server and doesn't know you. Jo has your context (email, calendar, files, conversations) and runs on your own machine.

Can I use jo on my phone?

Yes, through Telegram and WhatsApp. Text or voice. Same memory, same context as the Mac app. Native mobile app is coming soon.

Which Mac?

Any M-series chip (M1 through M4) with 16GB RAM. Most Macs from 2020 onwards.
