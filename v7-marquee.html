Frequently Asked Questions

What is jo and how does it work?

jo is a local AI assistant for Mac that indexes your notes, photos, emails, and files. It runs entirely on your computer and surfaces relevant information before you even search for it‚Äîlike having an AI with a perfect memory of your digital life.

How does jo know my stuff instantly?

jo connects directly to Notes, Photos, Mail, and your files‚Äîthe same way Apple apps talk to each other. It indexes everything locally, so it's ready to help the moment you install it.

Does my data ever leave my Mac?

No. jo runs 100% on your computer. Your notes, photos, and emails never get uploaded anywhere. Nothing is shared. Nothing leaves your machine. Ever.

How is jo different from Apple's search or Siri?

Apple search matches keywords. jo understands meaning. Ask for "that email about the budget issue" and jo finds it‚Äîeven if you never used the word "budget." It's like Siri, but useful.

How does jo pricing work?

No subscription. Simple queries (finding a note, searching photos) cost ~1 cent. Complex queries that synthesize multiple sources cost ~5 cents. Most users spend $3-8/month.

Which Mac do I need for jo?

Any Mac with an M-series chip (M1, M2, M3, M4) and 16GB of memory. That's most Macs from 2020 on. The M-series Neural Engine makes local AI fast and efficient.

Will jo slow down my Mac?

You won't notice it. jo uses dedicated AI hardware (the Neural Engine) that sits idle most of the time anyway. It's designed to run efficiently in the background.

Is jo free to use?

The jo app is free to download. You only pay for queries on a per-use basis‚Äîno subscriptions. Most users spend less than $8/month, and simple queries cost just pennies.
