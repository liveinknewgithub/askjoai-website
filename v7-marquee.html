The technology behind jo

Local vector embeddings

jo converts your content into semantic vectors stored entirely on your Mac. This is how it understands "money problems" means "budget" without keyword matching.

Apple Neural Engine

M-series Macs have dedicated AI hardware that sits idle 99% of the time. jo uses this chip for fast, efficient local processing.

Native macOS APIs

jo reads your data the same way Apple's own apps do. No hacks, no workarounds, no screen recording.
