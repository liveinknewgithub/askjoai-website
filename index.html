Common questions

What data does jo access?

jo can index your local files, emails (via IMAP), and browser history. You control exactly what jo sees, and everything stays on your Mac.

Is my data sent to the cloud?

Never, unless you explicitly choose to. jo runs entirely on your Mac. When you call in a cloud model like GPT, only that specific conversation is sent.

Why does it require an M-series Mac?

Apple's M-series chips have a Neural Engine that makes local AI fast and efficient. jo uses this power to run models directly on your hardware.

How much do cloud models cost?

Pay-per-use, typically $0.01-0.05 per conversation. No subscriptions. Most users spend less than $5/month, and local queries are always free.
