Questions? We've Got Answers

How does the local/cloud choice work?

Every conversation, you pick: "Local" keeps everything on your device (100% private), or choose a cloud AI like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Grok when you want their specific strengths.

When should I use local vs cloud?

Use local for anything sensitive - finances, health, personal stuff, business secrets. Use cloud when you need cutting-edge capabilities or want a second opinion from different AIs.

Do I need accounts for cloud AIs?

You can use your existing accounts or API keys for ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Grok. Local mode works without any accounts at all.

Is my data safe when using cloud mode?

In cloud mode, your messages go to the AI provider you choose (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, or xAI). We don't store or see any of it. You deal directly with them.

Can I use it without internet?

Yes! Local mode works completely offline. Cloud mode obviously needs internet, but you can always fall back to local when you're disconnected.

What does it cost?

Jo is free to download. Local AI is always free with unlimited use. Cloud AIs (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok) cost a small fee per chat - you only pay for what you send to them.
