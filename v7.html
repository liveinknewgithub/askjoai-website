Questions

How does jo know my stuff instantly?

jo connects directly to Notes, Photos, Mail, and your files—the same way Apple apps talk to each other. It indexes everything locally, so it's ready to help the moment you install it.

Does my data ever leave my Mac?

No. jo runs 100% on your computer. Your notes, photos, and emails never get uploaded anywhere.

How is this different from Apple's search?

Apple search matches keywords. jo understands meaning. Ask for "that email about the budget issue" and jo finds it—even if you never used the word "budget."

Why is this free?

jo runs on your Mac using AI hardware you already own. No servers, no ongoing costs to us. We're building something we wanted to exist.

Which Mac do I need?

Any Mac with an M-series chip (M1, M2, M3, M4) and 16GB of memory. That's most Macs from 2020 on.

Will this slow down my Mac?

You won't notice it. jo uses dedicated AI hardware that sits idle most of the time anyway.
