Questions

Is this actually native or just another Electron app?

SwiftUI, top to bottom. We care about this as much as you do.

Will running AI slow down my Mac?

jo uses the Neural Engine—dedicated hardware that's separate from your CPU and GPU. Your Mac won't break a sweat.

How does local AI compare to GPT-5.2?

For questions about your own files and context, local is often better—it has information cloud models don't. For complex reasoning, cloud models are more capable. That's why jo gives you both.

What's the catch? Why is this free?

Local features are free forever. We make money when you use cloud models—pennies per query, passed through at cost.

What about privacy?

Local queries never leave your Mac. Period. Cloud queries go directly to the provider—we don't store them.

What models are supported?

Local: qwen3-4b now, more coming. Cloud: GPT-5.2, Claude 3.5 Sonnet, and others. You choose per-query.
